ABOUT US

ExoFly denotes the exoskeleton for flight.The aim of this project is to foster the development of a safe, quiet, ultra-compact, near VTOL personal flying device, which is capable of flying 20 miles in one go. The device should carry a single person and function safely in both crowded cities and rural areas; it should be manoeuvrable enough so that anyone can move it around, and it should be quiet not only for the user but also for the general public.
A team of 20 young engineers is working day and night to achieve this goal at the earliest. We have been working to develop a 1/3rd scaled prototype where we could experiment and ensure all safety measures before we build upon the final model and we expect to build the prototype by the end of this year and have a ready-to-fly device by the end of next year!

Team Structure

Our team has both Business and Technical divisions which are further divided into Mechanical,Electrical and Controls.



1]Electrical :

Electrical Subdivision is responsible for :

1)Managing parameters of batteries.
2)Testing various motor-propeller configurations to find out the most efficient one.
Which Batteries are the best suitable fit and Why?
Lithium Polymer(LiPo) is a better choice than Lithium ion batteries Because of high current carrying capacity but also it needs more batteries to be connected together as its energy density is comparatively low. This results into increase in the weight of drone.

BMS(Battery Management system) board This board is used to monitor the voltage and temperature of each cell and can even balance voltage across separate cells.

Which motors to use for maximum efficiency of dones?
Brushless Direct current motors(BLDC) are used for hobby fliers in the quadcopter.

Exofly uses CRP i.e.contra-rotating propeller.Compared with conventional propellers the CRP achieves its optimum performance at a smaller diameter for a given rpm, or at lower rpm for the same diameter.The CRP also generally has a superior cavitation performance and reduced noise emission due to the lighter blade loading.Research is done in the team for optimal separation between the pairs of propellers.



2)Controls:

The Control subsystem basically acts as a connecting bridge with all the other subsystems(Mechanical and Electrical) for controlling the drone.The drone is a Decacopter(10 propellers) which is controlled using a pixhawk(it controls eight of the ten propellers) and an arduino board controlling the other two propellers.Pixhawk handles the vertical takeoff and landing(VTOL) perfectly with very high accuracy and maintaining a perfect 90 degree takeoff and land also pixhawk does it autonomously, whereas all the other features like dodging an object, object detection, etc can be easily implemented in an arduino board. The drone has 5 propellers one-above-another and for the stability of the drone, propellers rotate in opposite directions. We control user input to vehicle behavior in the most effective and friendly way. A feedback mechanism is made by connecting the microcontrollers with sensors like GPS,IMU and Proximity. For the simulation of the drone, the codes are written in a software known as Simulink and navigated in a software called Gazebo.Algorithms which are made for controls are feed to the pixhawk and the arduino board which uses the custom configuration called custom airframe which is the basic structure and configuration of the drone, the code is built on the framework and additional features are feed to the arduino board due to its flexibility over pixhawk.Due to Complexity and variations of the drone preconfigured flight-controllers are not used instead we make the custom airframe and code over it in Pixhawk and Arduino board.



3]Mechanical :

Mechanical subsystem is responsible for designing and manufacturing the device structure while ensuring that the device can bear all the structural and external loads.Mechanical subsystems support decision making regarding fabrication, experimental setup & material selection, final design and possible safety measures. This subsystem has to ensure the appropriate structure of device , including:

1]Dynamics & Testing
2]Design & Safety
3]Noise Control
